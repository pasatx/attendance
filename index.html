How to Use This Tool
1
Enter your address

Type your home address in the search box and pick from the suggestions, then press Search. You can also click directly on a house on the map — the tool will snap to the nearest address automatically.

2
See your 3 zoned schools

Your assigned elementary, middle, and high school appear as cards on the left. Each card shows the full school name, address, phone number, and links to the school website and enrollment portal.

3
Click a card to go to the school

Tap any school card to fly to that school on the map. A popup will open with its details.

4
Explore map controls

Click Map Controls in the sidebar to show or hide school markers, overlay attendance zone boundaries, or switch between Street, Satellite, and Topo basemaps. Use the home button on the map to reset the view.
